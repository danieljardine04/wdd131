Site Plan

Site Name: Memory Haven memoryhaven.com

I chose this name as a Journaling site because a Journal is where you want to keep all of your memories, A Haven is a safe place so Memory Haven is a safe place for your memories.

Site Pupose:

The site purpose is to create a journaling experience online for those who do not want to use social media.

Scenarios

Scenario 1:

I am a person that is tired of social media it is taking up too much of my time. I want a place to keep my adventures, but I don't want to have to go to a social media platform to do so.

Scenario 2

I am a person that used to do Journaling a lot when I was younger. Today there is not as much time to sit down and write all of my experiences like before. I would like a place online where I can keep all my experiences.
